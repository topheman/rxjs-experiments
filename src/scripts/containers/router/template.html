Router

I needed a frontend router with the following features:

  • history support (back button)
  • mutiple routes / customizable routes
  • deferred mounting (only mount a route when a promise is resolved)
  • params support (optional)

Frameworks like AngularJS, EmberJS, React-Router (not a framework) provide that out of the box. This site doesn't use any of them (by choice - I wanted to test RxJS outside of any framework).

So, I made a micro-router myself, based on history - it's supported by unit-tests.
